About Us

The conflict in the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) began in the mid-1990s following regional instability after the 1994 genocide in Rwanda. Since then, repeated wars and ongoing armed violence—especially in eastern Congo—have deeply affected civilians. Millions of families have been displaced, and children have suffered the most, facing loss of education, healthcare, and basic safety. Despite peace efforts, insecurity continues today, leaving many children vulnerable to poverty, hunger, and trauma. Saving Congo (DRC) Kids was created to bring hope where it is needed most—by supporting education, healthcare, and protection for children impacted by years of conflict. We believe every child deserves safety, dignity, and a chance for a better future.
